Why Digital Coupons?

We have noticed a few disadvantages of conventional coupons.

  • Lack of transparency - A lot of the current implementations of food coupons are based solely on trust. Donors usually entrust food vendors with preparing and distributing food for students.
  • Hard to track - It is hard to track how these coupons are distributed and redeemed.
  • Open to abuse - These coupons are susceptible to forgery and misuse.
  • Inefficient - Paper is fragile. Destroyed or lost coupons are wasted and can no longer be benefited.
  • The time and effort required to process these coupons discourage vendors from accepting them as it affects their cash flow.

How It Works

  1. You buy food coupons.
  2. Coupons are generated in the form of URLs.
  3. You distribute these URLs (coupons).
  4. Students open the URL on their phone displaying a QR code of the coupon.
  5. Food vendors scan the QR code and claim the coupon.
  6. Coupons are reimbursed directly into the vendor's bank account.
