Why Digital Coupons?

Transparent

You are free to give the coupons you buy to any student. Plus, every time a coupon is spent, you will be notified.

Convenient

Digital coupons can be easily distributed through online channels like email and WhatsApp. Students can immediately spend the coupons at any of the participating food vendors nearest to them and at a time most convenient to them.

Efficient

As transactions happen online, food vendors get paid faster. This will encourage more vendors to participate and in turn help local businesses.

How It Works

  1. You buy food coupons.
  2. Coupons are generated in the form of URLs.
  3. You distribute these URLs (coupons).
  4. Students open the URL on their phone displaying a QR code of the coupon.
  5. Food vendors scan the QR code and claim the coupon.
  6. Coupons are reimbursed directly into the vendor's bank account.
